Common Questions

Before You
Reach Out

Some of the most common enquiries our concierge team receives — answered here so your stay begins with perfect clarity.

Standard check-in is from 14:00 and check-out by 12:00 noon. Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged at no additional charge, subject to availability — simply notify our concierge team in advance.

Yes. We provide complimentary private transfers for guests in our Presidential Suite category, and a chauffeured airport transfer service for all other suite types at a flat rate. Our driver will meet you at the arrivals hall with a Haylo card. Please share your flight details at least 24 hours in advance.

Every dietary requirement and preference is accommodated without exception. Our kitchen team works directly with guests to design personalised menus. Please communicate your requirements to the concierge at least 48 hours before arrival and we will ensure everything is arranged perfectly.

The Haylo Spa accepts a limited number of day-guest appointments each week, available exclusively by prior reservation. We strongly recommend booking a minimum of two weeks in advance, as availability fills quickly. Please contact our spa team directly via reservations@haylohotel.com.

Cancellations made more than 72 hours before check-in incur no penalty. Cancellations within 72 hours are subject to a one-night retention charge. For stays of five nights or more, a 7-day cancellation window applies. Presidential Suite bookings have a 14-day policy. Full terms are outlined at point of reservation.
